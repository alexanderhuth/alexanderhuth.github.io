# Organic Growth Advisor

I’m a Berlin-based consultant, advisor, and mentor focused on sustainable organic growth through SEO and content marketing.

With a proven track record of building, leading, and supporting high-performing SEO teams, I help organisations improve how SEO is run — enabling operational excellence and stronger, more predictable search performance.

I work with in-house SEO teams and leadership, venture capital firms, investors, and boards, as well as agencies and consulting firms — adapting my approach to each organisation’s capabilities, needs, and ambitions.

## How I Help

I help organisations deliver critical objectives, strengthen teams for the next stage of growth, and create clarity around SEO performance and effectiveness.

### Due Diligence

Gain a clear, unbiased view of SEO performance, risks, and opportunities to support confident decisions.

- Assess SEO strategy, objectives, and alignment with business goals
- Evaluate technical SEO foundations, site architecture, and scalability
- Review content strategy, quality, and organic growth potential
- Analyse performance data, KPIs, and reporting quality
- Identify execution gaps, risks, and dependencies across teams and processes
- Review tools, workflows, and operational maturity
- Evaluate team setup, roles, and decision-making structures
- Assess current and potential use of AI across SEO strategy, workflows, and tooling
- Highlight quick wins, structural improvements, and growth opportunities
- Provide clear recommendations and priorities for the next 6–12 months
{: .consulting-list}

### Operational Excellence

Optimise your SEO function with structured strategy, measurable goals, effective processes, and empowered teams that deliver consistent business impact.

- Develop and refine SEO and content strategies aligned with business goals
- Translate strategy into clear planning and execution to deliver defined objectives
- Define measurable objectives and key results (OKRs/KPIs)
- Establish reliable reporting to provide clarity on performance and progress
- Improve communication with leadership, teams, and stakeholders
- Build capability through targeted training and up-skilling
- Manage cross-functional dependencies to reduce friction and delays
- Optimize tools, dashboards, and workflows
- Integrate AI responsibly to improve research, workflows, and decision-making
- Strengthen team structures to support sustainable delivery and growth
{: .consulting-list}
